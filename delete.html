Account & Data Deletion Request

We respect your right to delete your account and associated data. This page explains what data we have and how to request deletion.

What Data We Store

  • Apple Sign-In ID: Used to identify your account for premium subscriptions
  • Subscription Status: Managed through RevenueCat/Apple
  • Local Scan History: Stored only on YOUR device (not our servers)

What We DON'T Store

  • Your scanned photos (processed in real-time, then deleted)
  • Your name or email address
  • Payment information (handled by Apple/Google)

Request Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, please send us an email with the subject "Delete My Account". Include the email address associated with your Apple ID or Google account.

We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm deletion via email.

Request Deletion via Email

Delete Local Data

Your scan history is stored locally on your device. To delete it:

  • Open TrueCancer app
  • Go to Profile tab
  • Tap "Delete Account" or "Clear History"

Cancel Subscription

To cancel your premium subscription:

  • iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → TrueCancer → Cancel
  • Android: Play Store → Payments & Subscriptions → Subscriptions → TrueCancer → Cancel
