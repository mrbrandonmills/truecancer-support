Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 16, 2025

TrueCancer ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Photos: When you scan a product, the photo is sent to our secure servers for AI analysis. Photos are processed in real-time and are NOT permanently stored on our servers.

Scan History: Your scan history is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your scan history unless you choose to share it.

Account Information: If you subscribe to TrueCancer Pro, your purchase is processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide product safety analysis
  • To improve our AI analysis accuracy
  • To process subscription purchases through Apple
  • To respond to support requests

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data during transmission. All API communications use HTTPS encryption.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude AI: For product analysis (photos are processed but not stored)
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management
  • Apple App Store: For payment processing

Data Retention

Product photos are processed in real-time and deleted immediately after analysis. We do not maintain a database of your scanned products on our servers.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your local scan history at any time within the app
  • Cancel your subscription through the App Store
  • Request information about data we may have collected

Children's Privacy

TrueCancer is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
