FAQ

  • How does TrueCancer work?
    Our AI analyzes product labels and ingredients to provide safety scores based on scientific research and regulatory databases.
  • Is my data private?
    Yes. Photos are processed securely and not stored on our servers. See our Privacy Policy.
  • What products can I scan?
    Food, beverages, cosmetics, cleaning products, cookware, supplements, and more.
  • How do I restore my subscription?
    Go to the app, tap your profile icon, and tap "Restore Purchases".
